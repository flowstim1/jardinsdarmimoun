Our History

History in Every Corner

Dar Mimoun restaurant is located in the former residence of the Pacha of Marrakech. In an authentic setting, the open-garden lounge with its hand-painted cedar wood roof and exclusive blue zellige welcomes you for a timeless moment.

The Pacha's Residence

Created exclusively for the Pacha of Marrakech, featuring a hand-painted cedar wood roof and signature blue zellige tiles.

Historical Soul

A place with an authentic soul that pierces time, having been one of the former residences of El Glaoui.

Dar Mimoun - House of Luck

A gastronomic sanctuary perpetuating the Pacha's standards, where kindness and authentic flavors meet.
